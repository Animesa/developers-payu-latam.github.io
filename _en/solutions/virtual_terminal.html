Virtual terminal

 
With this feature, you can receive payments in person or by phone.

Your client will provide you with the necessary information to complete the transaction and you will immediately receive a confirmation of the purchase.

You do not need to have a website to receive payments securely.

Note:
  • This product is temporarily available only for accounts in Colombia, Mexico y Brazil.
 
To use the Administrative Module:  
01. go to http://www.payulatam.com/, At the top, you will find the
"Login to Your Account" field. There you can enter your respective username and password, which will have been supplied via email at the time of creation of your PayU Latam.account
02. To process charges you must enter the “Tools” tab of the “Online Processing”option
03. You must enter the required information to make the payment. The fields may vary according to the country and the means of payment available.
04. When you are finished completing the form, you will see the consolidated data entered.
05. Once you submit the payment data, you will receive a notification from the system regarding the outcome of the transaction. Additionally your customer will receive an email with the payment information.
