Payment request

 
 
 
 
 
With payment requests that you generate from your administrative module, you can make charges without having a website.

To receive payments with this solution all you need is an active account in PayU.

You can choose to receive a total payment or send your customers a detailed description of the sale. You just need to send your request via email or generate a link that you can share in chat rooms or social networks. Once your customer receive it, they can make the payment through our secure payment system.
 
 
The options you have to receive payments without having a website are:  
  • Simple payment request
  • Detailed payment request
