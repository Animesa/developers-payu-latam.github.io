TRANSFER TO A BANK ACCOUNT  
This option allows you to make fund transfer requests of the available balance in your PAYU account, to the bank account you registered at the time of affiliation with PAYU. (Also called ACH withdrawal)
You can access this feature in the “Accounts” menu by clicking on the “Transfers” option.

You must select the account with which you want to make the transfer, and the value to be transferred to your bank account.

Once you make the transfer, you will receive confirmation with additional information indicating the cost and the estimated time for transfer.

Keep in mind:
If your PayU account is from Brazil, but you want to transfer money to banks in other countries, you must select the account and date range when making the operation. The system will calculate the balance that can be transferred to the bank account, according to the transactions you make in that time period.

  • There is no limit in the date range to make the transfer.
  • The transfers must be consecutive, if the first transfer was performed in the 01/01/2014 to 01/31/2014 range, the next transfer date must start on 02/01/2014.
  • •The consolidated balance shown does not include the amount of the transfer’s discount.
 
 
TRANSFERS TO PAYU ACCOUNTS  
You can make transfers between available PayU accounts with this feature. You can also make transfers to other accounts of stores affiliated with PayU.
It is important to consider the following:
  • If you do not have this option enabled you must write to sac@payulatam.com and request this feature be activated.
  • You can only make transfers between accounts with the same associated currency.
 
Nota: The option of transfers to PayU accounts is available for accounts in all countries; the transfers must be done between accounts of the same country.
You can access this feature in the “Accounts” menu by clicking on the “Transfer” button..

Next, click on the “Transfer to PayU Accounts” button..

The module allows you to choose the account from which you want to transfer and displays the available balance of that account. • You must enter the account number to which you want to transfer the balance and once confirmed the system will show you the name of the holder to whom the account belongs. • The system will then let you enter a reference number to identify the transfer you wish to complete (this field is not mandatory). • The system will prompt you to enter the value you want to transfer to the account and will display the value of the commission to be charged.

The system will then ask you to verify the data and confirm the transfer.

Finally you will see a window with the details of the transaction. Additionally you will receive an email with a summary of the withdrawal.
